## Grounded conclusions

### FACT

- The fetch/normalize/pair pipeline produced real artifacts at limit 100 and
  limit 1000.
- The limit-1000 run produced 981 normalized swaps and 438 paired trades.
- The parser can mark uncertainty at scale instead of forcing a confident label.
- Pump.fun-like mints dominate the observed paired trades in this sample.

### INTERPRETATION

- The target wallet appears to behave like a short-horizon meme-token scalper in
  this window.
- Repeat entries suggest wave-level analysis is required, not only token-level
  analysis.
- Positive PnL with 50% win rate suggests skew / tail-winner behavior, not a
  high-hit-rate strategy.

### HYPOTHESIS

- The wallet may have an edge in filtering poor candidates rather than in a
  single positive entry trigger.
- Pump.fun lifecycle / graduation / second-wave behavior may be relevant.
- Cluster, execution, or off-chain context may explain entries better than paired
  trades alone.
